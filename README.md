# TicTacToe_android_with_basicML

Tic-tac-toe is a paper-and-pencil game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game. This apps lets you play against the machine which is powered with artificial intelligence and leaves a rare chance for you to win.You can also play this game in multiplayer mode.
